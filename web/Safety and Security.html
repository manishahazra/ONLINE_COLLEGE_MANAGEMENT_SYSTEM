Safety and Security Committee


Centered and Justified Text

A safety and security committee can have different roles depending on the context, such as in a workplace, college, or sports event: • Workplace A safety committee's main role is to ensure workplace safety. They can: • Develop, implement, and monitor health and safety policies and procedures • Train employees • Receive and investigate safety concerns from workers • Review incidents, employee complaints, and statistical records • Conduct inspections and interviews • Make recommendations to the agency head • College A College safety and security committee reviews all aspects of a child's safety, including: • Physical safety • Emotional and personal safety • Social safety • Emergency preparedness • Cyber safety • Audits, proactive measures, and corrective measures • Sports event The Committee on Safety and Security at Sports Events (T-S4): • Makes recommendations to parties on measures to implement the Convention • Offers technical assistance • Facilitates the collection and exchange of experience and good practice between States Other types of safety and security committees include: • IOGP: Promotes a safe and secure operating environment by implementing threat and risk assessment processes • OpenAI: Presents recommendations to the company's full board on critical safety and security decisions • OpenAI's independent safety board: Can delay model launches over safety concerns
