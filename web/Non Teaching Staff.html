Non Teaching Staff


Centered and Justified Text

The non teaching staff refers to those people who work at a college but don't engage in teaching the students. The examples of non teaching staff are receptionists, librarians, accountants, peons, human resource managers, counselors, cafeteria members, office staff, cleaning staff, etc. Non-teaching staff are employees in educational institutions who do not have instructional responsibilities. The definition of non-teaching staff can vary by country. Some examples of non-teaching staff include: • Administrators, such as headteachers and principals • Supervisors • Counselors • College psychologists • College health personnel • Librarians or educational media specialists • Cleaning and catering personnel • Minders • Teaching support personnel, such as speech therapists, physiotherapists, auxiliary teaching technicians, and pedagogues Non-teaching staff support the teaching system and are not directly involved in teaching
