Centered and Justified Text

There are multiple matches for PF committee, including the Central Board of Trustees (CBT) and the Regional Committee for EPF: • Central Board of Trustees (CBT) The CBT is the governing body of the Employees' Provident Fund Organization (EPFO). The CBT is made up of the following members: • Chairman • Vice-Chairman • 5 Central Government representatives • 15 State Government representatives • 10 Employers' representatives • 10 Employees' representatives • Central Provident Fund Commissioner • Member Secretary • Regional Committee for EPF The Regional Committee is made up of the following members: • Chairman • 2 State Government representatives • 2 Employers representatives • 2 Employees representatives • Non Official members of CBT ordinarily resident in the State The term of each Regional Committee is three years from the date of notification in the Official Gazette. The Chairman of the CBT is the authority to constitute or reconstitute the Regional Committee. The EPFO is under the administrative control of the Ministry of Labour and Employment, Government of India.
