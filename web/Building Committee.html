Building Committee


Centered and Justified Text

The Building Committee will be responsible for finalizing the plans and estimates of the various building projects proposed by the Commission and for ensuring the completion of the buildings in accordance to the approved plans, estimates and proper utilization of the fund. A building committee is a group of people who are responsible for the planning, construction, and maintenance of buildings. The role of a building committee can vary depending on the organization, but some common responsibilities include: • Planning: Advising on building activities, reviewing plans, and finalizing estimates • Construction: Ensuring that buildings are completed according to approved plans and estimates • Maintenance: Supervising maintenance and repair work • Outreach: Informing the public about the project's progress • Environmental: Promoting an eco-friendly campus and conserving natural resources Building committees can be made up of staff members or appointed members. They may also be responsible for: • Addressing member concerns • Scheduling events like open houses and parent-teacher conferences • Liaising with other organizations, such as the college board, fire department, or municipal authority When working with a building committee, it's important to consider the physical and non-physical environment in which the facility will be developed. This includes things like location, zoning, building code, and ADA.
