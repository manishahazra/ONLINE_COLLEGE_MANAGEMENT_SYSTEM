Kanyashree


Centered and Justified Text

Kanyashree is a scholarship scheme for girls in West Bengal, India that aims to improve their status and well-being: • Purpose To help girls from low-income families stay in school and delay marriage until the legal age of 18 • Components Includes an annual scholarship of Rs. 1,000 and a one-time grant of Rs. 25,000 • How it works Cash is paid directly into the girls' bank accounts, and they can decide how to use it • Benefits The scheme also aims to enhance the girls' social power and self-esteem through communication strategies • Recognition The scheme has been recognized by the United Nations Department of International Development and UNICEF The Kanyashree scheme also includes a Kanyashree Club, which provides a platform for girls to discuss topics such as education, society, and their bodies.
